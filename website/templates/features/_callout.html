Our Story

OMBU was founded in 2009 by Martin Rio, who drove across the country in a VW Bus with a laptop, a phone, and a love of technology. A few short years later in 2012, we launched a redesigned paramount.com for Paramount Pictures, proving that a small team of top talent can build big things on the web.

Since then, we’ve worked with clients like the Smithsonian Institute, Car Talk, Nike, and Stand for Children to come up with great ideas that turn into enterprise applications.
